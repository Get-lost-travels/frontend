Burano and Murano Boat Tour

A four-hour morning boat trip with lace-making and glass-blowing demonstrations

Description:

On this morning boat tour, you’ll get to sail into the Venetian Lagoon and see two beautiful islands in just four hours – Burano and Murano. During the boat ride, you’ll hear about the history of the islands through onboard guide commentary. Then, once you get to Murano, you’ll learn about its glass-making tradition and you’ll be able to experience a glassblowing demonstration. Similarly, in Burano, you’ll learn about another type of local craftsmanship – lace-making. You’ll be able to stop and watch a lace-making workshop before your tour finishes and you head back to shore.
